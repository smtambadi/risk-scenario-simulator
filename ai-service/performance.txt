Day 12 — Performance Benchmark

Tested 20 requests per endpoint

/query (AI response)
p50: 2000 ms
p95: 5000 ms
p99: 8000 ms

/query (cached response)
p50: 10 ms
p95: 60 ms
p99: 60 ms

/generate-report
p50: 300 ms
p95: 700 ms
p99: 750 ms

/report-status
p50: 100 ms
p95: 400 ms
p99: 400 ms

Conclusion:
- Query endpoint is slower due to AI processing time
- Redis caching significantly improves response time
- Async report generation reduces blocking and improves performance
- All endpoints are within acceptable performance limits
